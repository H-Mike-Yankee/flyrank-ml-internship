## 4. How the Claim Was Narrowed (Process Note)

The original draft claim combined two ideas ("AI-powered backend applications... and expanding expertise in production practices, cloud, MLOps"). Through interview-style pressure-testing, this was narrowed to one specific, defensible capability: **building backend APIs that integrate machine learning models into usable applications.** This was chosen because every supporting project follows the same underlying pattern — a trained or integrated ML model exposed through a FastAPI backend — making it a claim that can be explained in technical depth for the full length of an interview, rather than a broader statement that starts to fray under follow-up questions.
